Free Event! The Evolution of Beauty
at the Hammer Theatre May 14 at 7pm

Richard Prum’s academic career as an ornithologist and his lifelong passion for bird-watching come together in a thrilling intellectual adventure seeking to explain the incredible diversity of beauty in nature.

He is William Robertson Coe Professor of Ornithology at Yale University, and Head Curator of Vertebrate Zoology at the Yale Peabody Museum of Natural History. he has conducted field work throughout the world, and has studied fossil theropod dinosaurs in China. He received a MacArthur Fellowship in 2010.
